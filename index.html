The facts

A portrait of any alcohol offering company can be make up for the brands she carries. The specificity of our business is such that the vast majority of ingredients work with importers in the European market on an exclusive basis. That is, each importer has its own unique portfolio. Each importer is guided by a different logic when choosing the right ingredients, but in general this can be expressed by the priciple ´´the main thing is that is sells well.´´

Of course, we also realize that they should be sold. However, in the very logic of ingredient selection, we have our own clear philosophy that can be displayed in one phrase:´´we sell cocktails with a history´´, we select only top ingredients from families, small and medium sized, that focus on quality, rather than quantity.

Our mission, to put it briefly, is ´´to expand the cocktail horizons of our customers´´.We want to acquaint Dutch people with the most striking distinctive cocktails of Europe: to partially expand for the customer the assortment in the regions already known to him (Tuscany, Piedmont, etc.), but still to a greater extent reveal for him what was still left for him unknown.
